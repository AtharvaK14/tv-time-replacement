WatchTime — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 July 2026

WatchTime is a personal, local-first TV and movie tracker. It has no user accounts, no advertising, and no server operated by the developer. This policy explains, plainly, what happens to your data.

The short version

  • The developer does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any of your data.
  • Everything you create in the app stays on your device.
  • The only network requests go directly from your device to the metadata services listed below, using API keys that you provide.
  • No analytics, no ads, no trackers, no accounts.

Information stored on your device

Your library, watch history, ratings preferences, backup timestamps, app settings, and the API keys you enter are stored locally on your device (via the app's on-device storage). This information is not sent to the developer and is not accessible to anyone but you. It remains until you delete it.

Third-party services

To show posters, details, and ratings, the app sends requests directly from your device to the services below, authenticated with API keys you obtain and enter yourself. These requests include the titles and lookups you perform. The developer does not operate any intermediary server and never sees this traffic. Each service handles the requests it receives under its own privacy policy:

  • The Movie Database (TMDB) — privacy policy
  • OMDb API — terms & legal
  • TVmaze — privacy policy

Your API keys are stored only on your device and are transmitted only to the service they belong to, as part of making those requests.

Backups

The app can export a backup file containing your library and your API keys. That file is created and saved wherever you choose; the developer never receives it. Because it contains your API keys, keep it somewhere private.

Permissions

The app requests only internet access, which it needs to contact the metadata services above. It does not request access to your contacts, location, camera, microphone, files beyond the backups you explicitly create, or any other sensitive data.

Data retention and deletion

Because your data lives only on your device, you are in full control of it. You can erase it at any time using Settings → Reset Data in the app, by clearing the app's storage in Android settings, or by uninstalling the app, which removes its local data. Any backup files you exported are separate and can be deleted by you wherever you saved them.

Children

The app is not directed at children and collects no personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the “Last updated” date above will change accordingly. Continued use of the app after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
